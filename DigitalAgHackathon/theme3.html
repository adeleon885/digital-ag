üéì Learning from Experience: The "Quantification" Trap

The Trap: Measurement Uncertainty. Nori (blockchain carbon market) shut down in 2024 because quantifying soil carbon is scientifically hard and expensive. Their focus on solving transaction transparency and trust was ultimately not the critical bottleneck.

The Opportunity: Digital MRV. We need cheap, remote verification. Can you prove carbon storage without digging 1,000 holes?

The Trap: The "Yield Penalty." Climate-friendly farming often yields less in the first few years.

The Opportunity: Transition Finance. Tools that insure the farmer's risk during that specific transition window.

The Opportunity: Decision support tools. Tools that help farmers make decisions about climate-smart agriculture that protect their bottom line.

üß© Key Concepts

Mitigation: Reducing emissions (stopping the problem).

Adaptation: Planning and investing (evolving) for the changes that are already here or expected.

Resilience: Focuses on the system's ability to maintain core functions during a disturbance and recover quickly.

Extreme Weather: Statistical outliers (floods/heatwaves) that cause the most damage.

Agroecology: Harnessing ecological processes for resilience .
